About Me

I'm a twenty-something year old guy originally from the rolling green hills of South Wales. I am currently living in Albuquerque, New Mexico where I am a part of the University of New Mexico's track and field team while studying for an MBA. I'm very lucky to have the opportunity to live the life of an athlete in such an amazing location. The weather is beautiful, very different from what I'm used to, and the views are breathtaking. On top of that, the facilities are incredible and I have met some wonderful people. And I've only been there for one semester!
